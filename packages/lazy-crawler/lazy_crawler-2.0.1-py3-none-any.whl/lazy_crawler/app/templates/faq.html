Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Crawlio Intelligence

What's included in each pricing tier? +

The Standard (free) tier includes basic web scraping, community support, and open-source access. Pro Dashboard ($49/mo) adds unlimited collections, AI-powered analytics, live visualization, and priority support. Enterprise tier includes dedicated infrastructure, custom integrations, 99.9% SLA, and 24/7 dedicated support with custom pricing based on your needs.

How do you handle JavaScript-heavy websites? +

We use advanced Playwright integration to render JavaScript-heavy sites (React, Vue, Angular) in real browsers. This ensures we capture fully rendered content just as a human user would see it, handling dynamic loading, infinite scroll, and complex interactions seamlessly.

Is Crawlio compliant with Australian Privacy Act and CCPA/GDPR? +

Yes, absolutely. We're fully compliant with the Australian Privacy Act 1988, CCPA, and GDPR. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified, and we only collect publicly available data. We provide detailed compliance documentation and work with your legal team to ensure all requirements are met.

What data formats do you support? +

We deliver data in CSV, Excel, JSON, Google Sheets, and via webhooks/APIs. You can also integrate directly with your database (MongoDB, PostgreSQL) or data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery). Custom formats are available for Enterprise customers.

Do you have local teams in Australia and USA? +

Yes! We have dedicated support teams operating in Australian (AEST/AEDT) and US (EST/PST) time zones. This ensures you get timely responses during your business hours and that we understand regional compliance and business requirements.

How quickly can I start collecting data? +

For standard use cases, you can start immediately with our open-source tool. For custom enterprise solutions, we typically deliver first data samples within 48 hours of initial consultation. Full production deployment usually takes 3-5 business days depending on complexity.

Do you offer free trials for the Pro Dashboard? +

Yes! Contact us for a 14-day free trial of the Pro Dashboard. For Enterprise customers, we offer a 30-day pilot program with full access to all features to ensure it meets your requirements before commitment.
